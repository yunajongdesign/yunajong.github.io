    Tech Inspired. Style Redefined

    This 3D virtual-fitting room combines fashion and technology to push the boundaries of traditional ways of thinking. Unlike other virtual fitting rooms, shoppers can create 3D digital avatars of themselves using their own actual body measurements. An almost perfect digital representation of their virtual self to help shoppers define their personal style. This powerful layering technology allows shoppers to virtually play dress-up.

    Integration

     

    After the 3D scan, a few lines of codes; you are up and running. Our one-time installation process typically takes less than a day. The clothes will automatically be integrated into your product list, meaning you can easily launch, add or remove products.

    3D Scan 

    Digitize your clothing collection using our 3D Scanning Rigs. Our advanced scanning technology allows you to easily and efficiently digitize your garments into 3D for virtual fitting. You only need to scan one sample size, our technology does the rest. To produce this unique scanning animation, I had the pleasure to co-work with our 3D artists.

    Actionable Insights

     

    We provide the most intuitive user experience to help your operations, and give you the edge with unique insights into shopper’s patterns and behaviors. We aim to deliver optimized online shopping experience, reduce cost from returns, and increase conversation rates.
